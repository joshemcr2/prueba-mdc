Through the Open Door Grant, Miami Dade College is offering financial support for selected non-credit
programs that lead to industry certification. Tuition fees, exams, books, and materials will be covered
for students who need assistance to enroll in a short-term workforce program.

The Open Door Grant program is designed for Florida residents who would like to improve
their career opportunities.

Apply now and open the door to your success!
